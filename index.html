About Us

Karis music entertainment was officially launched on the 10th of october 2016. The record label was birthed as a result of the growing need to help young talented people actualize their dreams and provide suitable platforms for them to express their talents. The label officially started with four artiste signed up. And in 2018 six new artiste were signed, totalling the label signed artiste to ten. In the same year the label officially signed its first choreography group. And have since helped this young people develope their talents and actualising their dreams...
